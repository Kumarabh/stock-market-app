Visit Zerodha’s official website and sign up using your mobile number.

{{companyName}} Trading Software ({{companyName}} Trading Platforms)


{{companyName}} offers mobile, desktop and web trading platforms. Following are the trading platforms offered by {{companyName}}: We can trade with {{companyName}} in following ways:

Kite 3.0 (Web-based Trading Platform)

Launched in November 2015, Zerodha Kite 3.0 is a web based trading platform based on HTML 5 lightweight and fast trading platform. This trading platform can be used on mobile, tablets and on web-browsers. The platform was developed in-house by Zerodha and has undergone various updates since its inception. It offers full market watch, advanced charting with approximately 100 indicators and 6 types of charts.


Kite Android/ Kite iOS (Mobile Trading App)

It is a smartphone application for both Android and iOS users. It allows customers to trade from their mobile phones. The app is available for free for its customers and can be used for trading and monitoring stocks and commodities markets in the country.


Zerodha Console

Zerodha Console is a back-office platform and is a trade and reporting dashboard and can be used by customers to view and monitor all their investments including those made in stocks, derivatives, and mutual funds. Customers get access to various reports, get statements, and place withdrawal requests.


Zerodha Sentinel (Price Alert Tool)

It is a cloud-based tool offered by Zerodha which allows customers to set up price alerts on stocks, futures and options. The unique feature of this platform is that price alerts are triggered for any time, even when the system is off. Notifications are sent on Kite and on e-mail every time a price alert is triggered.


Zerodha Coin (Mutual Fund Investments)

Zerodha Coin is a mutual fund investment platform offered by the firm to its customers. Coin allows customers to invest in direct mutual funds which means that as a Zerodha customer you can invest in mutual funds on zero commission. It offers a saving of over 1.5% on commission every year.
