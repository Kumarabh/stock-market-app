AxisDirect Trading Software (AxisDirect Trading Platforms)

Axis Securities offer multiple trading platforms based on customers investment style and needs.


DirectTrade

A Desktop trading application which has advanced charting, auto-refreshing order/trade/position books and high frequency updates of market rate. This trading terminal is suitable for high frequency traders. The DirectTrade Terminal also offers live streaming quotes, Fully Customizable Multiple Market Watch and Quick access to order placement and reports. The DirectTrade is available at additional cost of Rs 2999 per year.


Swift Trade

A Java applet-based trading platform. This light-weight trading platform offers functionality of trading terminal in a web browser.


Mobile Trading

AxisDirect Mobile App allows customers to trade in Equity and Derivative segments through your smart phone.


AxisDirect lite

AxisDirect Lite is a low bandwidth, user friendly trading platform that allows customers to trade in Equities and Derivatives even on slower internet connection.
