Anand Rathi Trading Software (Anand Rathi Trading Platforms)

Anand Rathi offers 7 trading platforms to its customers:


Trade Lite

A website based online trading platform offering trading in equity and commodities & currency derivative segments. The trade lite website also offers features like other investment options like IPOs, free research reports, online portfolio etc.

Trade X'press

It's a browser based platform mainly designed for occasional investors. This platform is idle for those investors who believe in buy and hold approach towards investment in equities. It allows getting the updates real time. Also it gives instant research call Intimation to immediate action and instant Order/Trade confirmation. It works even over low bandwidth connectivity.

Trade X'pro

This platform is mainly suitable for active trader who does frequent transaction to capture favourable short-term price movements. It is an EXE based desktop software which offers dynamic charting, fully customizable display where add, delete and rearrange columns as per your viewing preferences, lock terminal option so you can lock your terminal when not in use and be assured that your account is not accessible to anyone but yourself.

Trade X'pro+

This platform offers 'Tools of the Trade' from AnandRathi that allows you to use tons of investment services, trading advice, news and trading tools. Below is the list of their featured products.

Nest Starter Pack: It's a free package that offers you intraday and historical charts, market monitor alert tool and market scanner which helps to shortlist stocks in real-time based on various conditions.

Nest Premium: It offers real-time news powered by Dow Jones streamed right into your terminal and also perform technical analysis on both short and long term charts to figure out the best time to buy and sell. It's free for one month and after that Rs 150 per month or Rs 1000 yearly.

Nest Pulse: It offers advanced charting with technical indicators, where you can build, test and execute your own trading strategies. It's free for one month and then Rs 125 monthly or Rs 899 yearly.

Vantage trade: In this system identifies emerging opportunities and generates unambiguous Buy/Sell signals for Delivery and Long/Short calls for Intraday/Delivery on all stocks across global exchanges.

Sim Trade: It's free and offers simulated trading environment to test out your strategies in cash and FO.

Web Chart: It allow viewing advanced intraday and historical charts on the website itself without the need to have a separate application. This feature is available for Rs 49 per month or Rs 399 annually.

Mundu TV: You can watch upto five business channels inside your terminal. This feature is available for Rs 49 per month or Rs 450 annually.

Heckly: Heckly provides Real Time Financial Information, News analytics and HeatMaps to get exclusive coverage of markets, companies and businesses. This feature is available for Rs 99 per month or Rs 600 annually.

Trade Flexi

Trade Flexi is available for intra-day trading in equity Cash & Future segment. This platform allows their investors to create intra-day position in the equity segment on leveraged margin along with securing the same position with a risk cover by placing a simultaneous stop loss market/limit order. It helps their investors to minimize the risk through system generated stop loss market/limit order.

Trade On Move

This platform gives flexibility to their investors to access account from anywhere. This is best suitable for those investors who are always on the move. Those can access trained dealers without incurring any STD cost. They have dedicated phone numbers so you can place your orders, get order confirmation and your account updates through directly access their Trade On move facility. Investor can also place After Market Orders through Trade On Move facility.

Trade Mobi

Trade Mobi supports Blackberry and Android devices. This platform allows their investors to access instantly and manages trades through mobile phone. Supported Exchange segment are NSE Cash, NSE F&O, BSE Cash, NSE Currency, NCDEX, MCX and MCX-SX.
