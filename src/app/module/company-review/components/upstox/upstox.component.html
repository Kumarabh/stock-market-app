Account Types

Upstox offers two different type of trading account to suite investors/traders need:

Upstox Basic Plan

Brokerage Free equity delivey and Rs 20 per trade for Equity F&O, Commodity and Currency The plan offers basic trading features in Equity Delivery segment through mobile and website. Upstock doesn't change any brokerage for trading in Equity Delivery segment. Upstox basic plan offers upto 20x leverage on Cash segment.

Upstox Priority Pack

Rs 0 equity delivey brokerage and flat Rs 30 per trade for Equity F&O, Commodity and Currency Upstox Pro is the trading platform for frequent traders; who trade in Indra-day and F&O segments. This plan offers upto 25x leverage on Cash segment. Along with the standard features offered in Upstox Basic Plan, Upstox Pro offers stock search, analyze, trade, and monitor stocks, currencies, F&O, and commodities. Upstox Pro provides advance charting for professional traders for technical analysis. It also offers wide range of indicators, chart types, data rich user interface.
