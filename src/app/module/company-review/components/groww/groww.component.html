Groww is a Bangalore based broker offering online flat fee discount brokerage services to invest in Equity, IPO, and Direct Mutual Funds. Groww has its own trading platform called Groww (web and mobile trading app) that offers a seamless trading experience to its investors. It is a safe and secure app with 128-bit encryption. Groww is one of India's fastest-growing platforms with a strong customer base of 90+ lakh users as of Nov 2020. It is also one of the highest-rated App (4.5+) in the Google Play Store and App Store.
