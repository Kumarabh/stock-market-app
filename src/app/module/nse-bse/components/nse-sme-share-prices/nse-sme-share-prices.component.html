NSE SME Share Price Live

NSE SME or NSE Emerge is the platform at the National Stock Exchange (NSE) for Small and Medium Enterprises (SME). SME companies in India can raise funds through the public and get listed at NSE by selling equities in the company via an SME IPO. The NSE Emerge SME stocks get traded on the same NSE exchange as normal companies.

SEM IPO's offer opportunity to the investors for early investment in businesses which are at a small scale. SME listed companies eventually move to the main exchange after a few years of growth.
