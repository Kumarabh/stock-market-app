NSE Bulk Deals

Bulk deal is a stock trading where stock quantities buy or sell by an investor is more then 0.5% of total number of equity shares of the company listed at India stock exchanges. In year 2004 SEBI bring more transparency to the bulk deals by making is compulsory for stock exchanges to publish intraday bulk deals at the end of the trading day.
